Refund and Cancellation Policy

A minimum 30 day(prior to date of arrival) notice is required to obtain full refund in case of cancellations. No exceptions. After the 30 day period expiry, full amount for your stay will be charged(non-refundable) Bookings made for 7 or more nights, will be required to deposit a minimum 7 nights deposit at the time of booking using your credit card ( Visa and Master Card only). For bookings made for less than 7 nights, a minimum of 2 nights deposit will be required. A minimum 7 nights stay is required for bookings between 15th July and 15th August(no particular days). 2 Adults and 2 children per site. Every extra Adult will be $10/night and every extra child will be $5/night. Thanks. Management.
