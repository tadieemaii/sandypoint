RULES

Campground Regulations
Please take a few moments to observe our campground rules. We want both you and your neighbor campers to have a safe and happy stay at our campground. Along with the friendly atmosphere at the campground, we would be grateful of you to follow the campground rules and regulations.
Quiet Times
  1. 11:00 P.M. - 7:00 A.M.
  2. No loud music at any time, no music after 11:00 P.M. Campers must be on their sites by 11:00 P.M. No beach use after 11 pm.
    Entrance Gate will be closed at 11 P.M.
Dogs
No Dogs permitted at any time, under any circumstances.
Refund and cancellation policy
A minimum 30 days (prior to date of arrival) notice is required to obtain full refund in case of cancellations. No exceptions. Bookings made for 7 or more nights will be required to make a deposit for a minimum of 7 nights at the time of booking.( Visa and MasterCard only). Bookings made for less than 7 nights will require a full deposit.
Fireworks
Fireworks are banned in the campground and surrounding area.
Beach
No glass containers on the beach. Don't use sand as an ashtray as all young children like to put everything into their mouths.There is no lifeguard on duty. Please supervise young children at all times. We do have a designated roped area for kids to swim. No campfires on the beach.
Visitors
You must register visitors at the gate office. All visitors must park in visitor parking area. Visitors must leave the campground prior to 10:00 P.M. or they will be charged for a night stay. Registered campers are responsible for their visitors' conduct and observance of campground rules.
Boaters And Water Skiers
  1. Stay outside the marked swimming area and observe all boating regulations.
  2. Boat launch is limited for usage by the campers only. Non-campers from outside are not allowed for their boat launch.
Valuables
Do not leave any valuables unattended. Although the campground has 24 hour security in place, you are ultimately responsible for your belongings.
Recycling
Please recycle - put it in blue recycle barrels, not in trash bins. Cardboard bins are only for cardboard - No garbage please.
Washrooms
We try really hard to keep them clean at all times; we hope you will appreciate our efforts. Please, no dishwashing in the shower building. If any emergencies arise and/or washrooms are in need of attention, please notify the office.
Motorbikes
Licensed for the road only are permitted. No off-road bikes are to be driven in the campground.
Parents
Are personally and legally responsible for their children's conduct and actions. All children must be back in their own site by 10:00 P.M.
Trees And Shrubs
Please help us keep our green spaces. Do not cut for wiener sticks, poles, etc.
Speed Signs
Please obey the posted speed limit of 10 km/hr inside the campground at all times. Speeding vehicles will be asked to park outside in the visitor parking.
Check-In Time
Anytime after 1:00 P.M. Rent is due and payable upon arrival. We accept credit cards( Visa and Mastercard only), debit cards and cash.
Check-Out Time Or Re-Registration
  1. By 10:00 A.M. Re-registration for your site is not guaranteed after 10:00 A.M.
  2. We reserve the right to refuse continued residency privileges of offenders of our campground policies.
  3. The operators of Sandy Point Campground will not be responsible for fire, theft, accidents to persons or property, vandalism or any act of God.
